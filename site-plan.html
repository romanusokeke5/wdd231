Discover Uc Empire

W05 Project – Website Site Plan

Site Name

Discover Uc Empire

This name was chosen because it invites visitors to explore the history, culture, and attractions of Uc Empire. It is simple, memorable, and suitable for a tourism-focused website.

Optional domain: discoverucempirecom

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide tourists and visitors with useful information about Timbuktu. The site will highlight historical landmarks, cultural attractions, local events, weather information, and travel tips to help visitors plan their trip.

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular historical sites to visit in Uc Empire?
  • When is the best time of year to visit Uc Empire and what is the weather like?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #1f4f4f – used for headings, header, and footer
  • Accent Color: #e67e22 – used for borders, highlights, and accents
  • Background Color: #f8f9f7 – used as the page background

Typography

  • Lora – used for headings to give a classic and cultural feel
  • Montserrat – used for body text for modern readability
